# Domain Catalog — Constraint Survey Reference (S2)

## Usage

Read this catalog when surveying candidate domains in S2 Step 2. For each domain, describe the problem constraint in domain-agnostic terms, then check whether the domain has a known solution pattern for that structural form.

## 25 Pre-Classified Domains

### 1. Evolutionary Biology
**Constraint-relevant description:** Selection pressure drives optimization under resource limits; co-evolution manages mutual dependencies.
**Known solution patterns:** Fitness landscapes, redundancy, adaptation rate tuning, niche specialization, symbiosis.
**Structural class:** optimization under constraint, competing requirements, long-horizon solutions.

### 2. Thermodynamics / Statistical Mechanics
**Constraint-relevant description:** Energy conservation and entropy increase bound all transformations; equilibrium and phase transitions are fundamental limits.
**Known solution patterns:** Heat engines (efficiency limits), phase transitions (discontinuous change), entropy as information, Maxwell demons (measurement cost).
**Structural class:** irreversibility, resource limits, hidden coupling between system states.

### 3. Fluid Dynamics
**Constraint-relevant description:** Flow adapts to boundary conditions; turbulence, laminar flow, and pressure gradients determine transfer rates.
**Known solution patterns:** Laminar/turbulent transitions, Bernoulli effects, vortex formation, channel optimization, valve logic.
**Structural class:** state-dependent behavior, path-dependence, bottleneck resolution.

### 4. Immunology
**Constraint-relevant description:** Self/non-self discrimination under incomplete information; adaptive response with memory; tolerance to internal variation.
**Known solution patterns:** Antigen-antibody specificity, clonal selection, memory cells, complement cascades, autoimmune failure modes.
**Structural class:** classification under uncertainty, adaptive specificity, memory systems.

### 5. Information Theory / Coding Theory
**Constraint-relevant description:** Communication under noise, bandwidth limits, and error probability; compression vs. redundancy trade-offs.
**Known solution patterns:** Shannon capacity limits, error-correcting codes, compression algorithms, channel coding, signal-to-noise ratios.
**Structural class:** information bounds, noise tolerance, encoding/decoding costs.

### 6. Architecture / Structural Engineering
**Constraint-relevant description:** Load distribution under material and geometric constraints; stability under dynamic loads.
**Known solution patterns:** Arches (compression distribution), trusses (triangulation), prestressed members, redundant load paths, tensegrity.
**Structural class:** load redistribution, distributed vs. concentrated stress, geometric solutions to force problems.

### 7. Ecology / Food Web Dynamics
**Constraint-relevant description:** Energy transfer across trophic levels; predator-prey cycles; keystone species and cascade effects.
**Known solution patterns:** Trophic cascades, keystone removal effects, resilience via diversity, carrying capacity.
**Structural class:** cascade effects, leverage points, network resilience.

### 8. Game Theory / Mechanism Design
**Constraint-relevant description:** Strategic interaction where multiple agents optimize individually; coordination failures, equilibria.
**Known solution patterns:** Nash equilibria, dominant strategies, mechanism design (aligning incentives), auction theory, cooperative games.
**Structural class:** multi-agent optimization, incentive alignment, coordination problems.

### 9. Developmental Biology / Morphogenesis
**Constraint-relevant description:** Complex form emerges from simple local rules and chemical gradients; differentiation from identical starting cells.
**Known solution patterns:** Reaction-diffusion systems, positional information (Turing patterns), cell fate determination, regeneration.
**Structural class:** emergence from local rules, self-organization, pattern formation without central control.

### 10. Control Theory / Cybernetics
**Constraint-relevant description:** Feedback-driven stability; control under delay, noise, and model mismatch.
**Known solution patterns:** PID control, Kalman filtering, Nyquist stability, robust control, model predictive control.
**Structural class:** feedback stability, robustness vs. performance trade-offs, delay compensation.

### 11. Crystallography / Materials Science
**Constraint-relevant description:** Atom arrangement determines bulk properties; symmetry breaking governs phase transitions.
**Known solution patterns:** Lattice structures, defect engineering (doping), grain boundary control, amorphous vs. crystalline phases.
**Structural class:** local structure → global property, symmetry breaking, defect leverage.

### 12. Network Science / Graph Theory
**Constraint-relevant description:** Connectivity patterns determine flow, robustness, and cascade failure risk.
**Known solution patterns:** Small-world networks, scale-free properties, percolation thresholds, community detection, spanning trees.
**Structural class:** connectivity leverage, critical thresholds, path optimization.

### 13. Linguistics / Syntax
**Constraint-relevant description:** Finite rules generate infinite structures; ambiguity resolution under context.
**Known solution patterns:** Recursive grammar, context-free/context-sensitive distinctions, disambiguation by context, pragmatics.
**Structural class:** finite means, infinite output, ambiguity resolution.

### 14. Epidemiology / Infection Dynamics
**Constraint-relevant description:** Spread through contact networks; threshold effects (R₀), herd immunity, containment vs. elimination.
**Known solution patterns:** SIR model, reproductive number control, quarantine logic, vaccination thresholds, contact tracing.
**Structural class:** threshold phenomena, network spread, intervention timing.

### 15. Acoustics / Wave Physics
**Constraint-relevant description:** Wave superposition, interference, resonance, and diffraction; energy transfer through media.
**Known solution patterns:** Resonance amplification, destructive interference (noise cancellation), standing waves, Doppler shifts, reflection/transmission boundaries.
**Structural class:** resonance leverage, destructive vs. constructive combination, boundary effects.

### 16. Economics / Market Microstructure
**Constraint-relevant description:** Price discovery under asymmetric information; liquidity constraints; externalities and public goods.
**Known solution patterns:** Auctions, price signals, Coase theorem (negotiation), Pigou taxes (externalities), commons management.
**Structural class:** information asymmetry resolution, externality internalization, distributed coordination.

### 17. Optics / Photonics
**Constraint-relevant description:** Light manipulation through refraction, reflection, interference; diffraction limits resolution.
**Known solution patterns:** Lenses, waveguides, interferometry, spatial filtering, holography, anti-reflection coatings.
**Structural class:** resolution limits, beam shaping, interference exploitation.

### 18. Psychology / Cognitive Science
**Constraint-relevant description:** Decision-making under cognitive load and bias; attention as a scarce resource; priming and framing effects.
**Known solution patterns:** Choice architecture (nudges), cognitive load reduction, habit formation, attentional cueing, error-recovery design.
**Structural class:** bounded rationality, attention scarcity, behavior change under constraint.

### 19. Pharmacology / Drug Design
**Constraint-relevant description:** Molecular targeting specificity; dose-response curves; ADME constraints (absorption, distribution, metabolism, excretion).
**Known solution patterns:** Receptor binding specificity, prodrug activation, targeted delivery, dose timing, synergistic combinations.
**Structural class:** specificity vs. selectivity, delivery constraints, timing-dependent effects.

### 20. Urban Planning / Traffic Flow
**Constraint-relevant description:** Flow optimization in shared infrastructure; congestion as emergent property of individual decisions.
**Known solution patterns:** Traffic signal coordination, induced demand effects, distributed routing (GPS), land use mixing.
**Structural class:** emergent congestion, system-vs-individual optimization, routing under shared resource.

### 21. Cryptography / Security
**Constraint-relevant description:** Information hiding under computational limits; trust establishment without shared prior knowledge.
**Known solution patterns:** Public-key infrastructure, zero-knowledge proofs, hash functions, protocol design, formal verification.
**Structural class:** asymmetric information leverage, trust bootstrapping, computational bounds as security.

### 22. Geology / Plate Tectonics
**Constraint-relevant description:** Slow accumulation of stress followed by rapid release; isostatic adjustment and long time horizons.
**Known solution patterns:** Fault zone dynamics, stress accumulation, elastic rebound, subduction cycles.
**Structural class:** slow accumulation / rapid release, threshold-based change, deep time effects.

### 23. Animal Behavior / Ethology
**Constraint-relevant description:** Fixed action patterns under triggering stimuli; cost-benefit foraging; social coordination without central authority.
**Known solution patterns:** Optimal foraging theory, sign stimuli, swarm intelligence, ritualized conflict, imprinting.
**Structural class:** stimulus-triggered automation, distributed coordination, conflict without full information.

### 24. Computation / Complexity Theory
**Constraint-relevant description:** Tractable vs. intractable problems; approximation trade-offs; space-time trade-offs.
**Known solution patterns:** Dynamic programming (subproblem reuse), randomized algorithms, approximation bounds, parallel decomposition.
**Structural class:** exponential vs. polynomial limits, approximation strategies, decomposition.

### 25. Chemistry / Catalysis
**Constraint-relevant description:** Reaction pathways have activation energy barriers; catalysts lower barriers without being consumed.
**Known solution patterns:** Transition state stabilization, enzyme kinetics (Michaelis-Menten), autocatalysis, reaction cascades.
**Structural class:** barrier-lowering without consumption, cascade amplification, selectivity at transition states.

## Survey Instruction (S2 Step 2)

1. Express the binding constraint in domain-agnostic terms (no vocabulary from the original problem domain)
2. Scan all 25 domains for "structural class" match to the agnostic description
3. For each matching domain: note the specific solution pattern that addresses the structural form
4. Generate domain mapping: [problem domain] → [catalog domain] via [structural analog]
